The Future of Household Security

The future of home security is convenience. Recent technological developments have resulted in “smart” systems that do much more than just sound an alarm when a sensor has been tripped. These new systems are connected to your thermostat, appliances, smartphone and computer. Now you have the power to monitor your home when you’re away.

With full-home control, you can arm and disarm your security system with a smartphone app, or a key fob, similar to what you would use to unlock your car. You can also conserve energy by changing the thermostat, turning off appliances and switching off lights.

Imagine the peace of mind you’d gain if you had the ability to log in to a website at work or on the road and arm your system, or check on the camera feeds to make sure everything is as you left it. It seems futuristic, but this technology is available now. Some home security companies, like ADT, already offer full-home control systems, with smartphone apps for iPhone and other mobile operating systems.
